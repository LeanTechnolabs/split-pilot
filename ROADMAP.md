# Roadmap

This roadmap is directional. It describes the current public product picture and how future items may be communicated. It does **not** represent delivery commitments, timelines, or guaranteed outcomes.

## Available now

Capabilities available in the public product today:

- Free to use
- No account or login required
- Private trip links for sharing a trip
- Create a trip, add members, and record expenses and transfers
- Each participant can represent one or more members who pay together
- Expense splits by equal share, parts, or exact amounts
- Participant balance calculations
- Recommended settlement transfers with quick record-payment links from the balances panel
- Trip start and end date fields on the trip form
- Multiple currency support
- Trip pages excluded from search indexing

## Being considered

Future public roadmap items will be listed here only after they are sufficiently defined for external communication.

No additional planned features are published in this section at this time.

## Not publicly committed

Anything not listed under **Available now** should be treated as not publicly committed. Ideas may be explored privately without appearing here, and items may be added, changed, or removed without notice.
